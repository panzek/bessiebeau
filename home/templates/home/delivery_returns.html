Delivery & Returns

Delivery

  • Standard delivery within Europe takes approximately within 5 to 6 working days (7-15 working days in selected countries outside Europe), this may vary by location and even product, and may be sent to you in different parcels;
  • All items purchased with us are sent to you directly by our merchants. Getting your order to you quickly is our priority and we’ve worked hard with our merchants to provide efficient delivery;
  • If you’ve purchased multiple items from different brands, you’ll receive a parcel from each of them. Don’t worry, you’ll get a confirmation email and text message, and tracking reference for each of your parcels which you can use to track when your goods will arrive.

Returns

  • You have 14 days from the date of delivery to return orders. Buyer pays return postage;
  • If you've received a damaged item, please contact us immediately and we'll do everything possible to help;
