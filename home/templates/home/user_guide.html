User Guide/Manual

This is a Quick User Guide (Manual) for the Customer. This explains to you the internal workings or make-up of the Bessie + Beau Shopping Mall as a Customer (Buyer).

Newsletter Sign Up:

  • Launch the site: bessiebeau.com and Sign up to Bessie + Beau Newsletter on the bottom of the page.

Register as a Customer:

  • Register for an account on Bessie + Beau using the Sign Up link at the top menu bar.

Login:

After your registration has been approved, you then login to “your account”. You will see the following:

  • Account Information
  • Address book
  • All other links under “My Account”

Make Purchase:

  • Buy any of the products on display. Happy Days!
